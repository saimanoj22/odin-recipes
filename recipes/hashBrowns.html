Asiago Hash Browns

What could be a worthy breakfast?

This savory blend of frozen hash browns, sour cream, and Asiago cheese makes a great potato side dish.

The creamy blend of hash brown potatoes and Asiago cheese makes a great side dish, or may even be served for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 (30 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
  • 1 ½ cups grated Asiago cheese
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
  • 8 fluid ounces heavy cream
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • paprika to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium casserole dish, mix the hash browns, Asiago cheese, onion, sour cream, heavy cream, and butter.
  3. Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until hash browns are fully cooked and the top is lightly browned. Season with paprika, salt, and pepper.
